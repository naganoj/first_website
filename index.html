About Me

I am a teacher at Western Reserve Academy. I teach computer science classes. My students build their technical and problem-solving skills in my classes.

One course that I teach is Computer Science 1. In this course, students learn web development skills and become familiar and proficient with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

When I am not teaching classes, I spend most of my time with my two daughters: Melanie, age 8, and Grace, age 6. They like going swimming, playing games, and reading.

Western Reserve Academy

Western Reserve Academy is a residential high school located in Hudson, Ohio.

This year, it was ranked #1 Private School in Ohio by Niche.

I love teaching at Western Reserve Academy because the students are fun, kind, hard-working, and talented. My colleagues are terrific, and the campus is beautiful.
